Registration Fee and Payment Methods

 

=============================================================================================================================

Registration Fee

Registration fee will be used to cover tea breaks, lunch, and banquet.
All professors and postdocs must pay registration fee; otherwise the registration is not considered valid.

Registration fee paid before April 20, 2018 is listed below

-- 1200 RMB (or 200 US dollars) for postdocs and faculty
-- 0 RMB (or 0 US dollars) for graduate students
-- 200 RMB (or 30 US dollars) for each accompanying person to attend the banquet.

For accompanying persons

 Accompanying person can attend the banquet. Please send an email to leeastronomy@sjtu.edu.cn to indicate the number of accompany persons and their names before April 20, 2018.


Payment Method

-- online e-payment via SJTU online payment (foreign CC with surcharge)

-- Please refer to SJTU payment tutorial in case of problems.

 

Receipt and Invoice

It takes 24-48 hours for the fund to be transferred and show up in our local account, thus it may take a few days for the payment confirmation letter to be sent via email. We apologize for the delay.

In addition to the confirmation letter, we will also provide a paper receipt (with official stamp) upon request via email or at the conference. If you need a specialized format or wording/title for the receipt, please notify leeastronomy@sjtu.edu.cn in advance.

 

Refund Policy

If written/email cancellation is received by April 20, 2018, a full refund (minus any applicable online payment surcharge) will be issued via the original means of payment.

If written/email cancellation is received after April 20, 2018 and before the first day of conference, a full refund minus 20% administrative charge (and any applicable online payment surcharge) will be issued via the original means of payment.

If written/email cancellation is received after the first day of conference or you do not attend the conference, no refund will be issued.
